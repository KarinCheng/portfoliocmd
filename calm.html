Calm

  • Client:

    School Project

  • Product:

    IOT Device

  • Category:

    Sleep

  • Product timeline:

    3 months

Challenge

There are so many people in the world who have trouble sleeping. Now with smartphones, people are exposed to more blue light than ever. 71% of the people who own a mobile phone usually sleep with it or have it next to them which can negatively impact their sleep schedule. Our task was to create a product that would help people sleep better.

Solution

We created a IoT watch for people who suffer from sleep paralysis. They wear it when they are going to sleep. The watch provides the sleep paralysis victim with a sense of security. The data that the watch collects is displayed in a app that is connected to WiFi.

View the website

Watch

The watch measures the user's heart rate and vibrates whenever it detects any irregularities as these are sign of sleep paralysis. This should help rouse the user from their paralyzed state, because once they are able to move a muscle they can break free.

App

The app displays information about their heart rate and when they experienced sleep paralysis. This information can then be taken to their doctor for further study.
