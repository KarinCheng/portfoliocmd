Meer Dan Vier Poten

  • Client:

    Personal Project

  • Product:

    Landing page

  • Category:

    Food

  • Project timeline:

    3 months

Challenge

Meer Dan Vier Poten is a food truck that serves dishes with creatures that have more than four legs. The truck is mostly meant for people who are 80 years old and above. They also wanted the design in a psychedelic style. The task is to design a landing page that would fit Meer Dan Vier Poten. Every element on the page has to be coherent.

Solution

We created a new branding that suits Meer Dan Vier Poten. Throughout the whole landing page remains contrast, consistency, alignment and proximity.
