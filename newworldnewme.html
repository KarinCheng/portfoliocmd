New World, New Me

  • Client:

    Personal Project

  • Product:

    Virtual Reality

  • Category:

    Students, School

  • Project timeline:

    3 months

Challenge

Brandstof is a cafe where people can order a drink. The Brandstof at the Amsterdam University of Applied Science wanted a product that would attract more students.

Solution

We designed a place for students to help them when they are stuck with their homework. With VR technology we created a digital space of the wood. They can freely walk around on a 360 treadmill. By using this product students are able to free their mind and get new inspiration.
