Welcome To Amsterdam

  • Client:

    Personal Project

  • Product:

    App

  • Category:

    Students, School

  • Project timeline:

    3 months

Challenge

International students who come to study at Amsterdam often do not know how our society works. They may feel disconnected from other people. Our task was to create something that would help those students feel like they are not forgotten in this big city.

Solution

We created an app that motivates students to study. It might be hard for them to concentrate when everything in their surrounding has changed. In the app they can set a timer to study. The app comes with a built-in timer to help students improve their studying habits. Every time they finish a session without using their phone, they are rewarded with points which can be used to get discounts on local attractions.
